Buttermilk Oatmeal Pancakes

Description

Classic pancakes with oatmeal variation in them. All the way from the allrecipes website!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 ½ cups rolled oats
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup cornmeal
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  •  ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix buttermilk and oats in a large bowl
  2. Rest about 5 minutes
  3. Stir flour, cornmeal, baking powder, sugar, baking soda, and salt into oat mixture
  4. Add eggs and vegetable oil; mix until just combined
  5. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium-high heat
  6. Drop batter by large spoonfuls onto the griddle
  7. cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 3 minutes
  8. Flip and cook until browned on the other side, about 3 minutes more
  9. Repeat steps 6-8 with remaining batter
